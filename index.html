Questions

Good to know

Is there a long-term contract?
No. You pick the commitment length up front — 2, 3 or 4 months. After that you're free to continue month-to-month or stop. Day passes and the holiday membership have zero ongoing commitment.
What does "signup fee + last month" mean?
A one-time joining fee plus your final month paid in advance, so your membership is always covered through your last day. Shorter plans have a higher signup fee; the 4-month plan has $0 signup.
Can I really try it for free?
Yes — the 7-Day Guest Pass is free for first-time visitors aged 18+ living within 10km. No card required to start.
Do I get access to classes and recovery?
All ongoing memberships include group classes and the recovery zone (sauna + stretching) at no extra cost.
How do I cancel?
Cancel anytime from your member portal once your committed term is complete. No phone calls, no retention runaround.
